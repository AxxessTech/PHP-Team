# Axxess PHP Team

Most of our projects are not built or maintained by a single person. Instead, there is a collection of people involved, who all have their own personal preferences. If everyone used their own personal style, maintainability would suffer.

These guidelines are considered a living document. People, teams, and opinions change over time. We don't blindly follow the rules we agreed on in the past, but keep challenging them. New experiences lead to new and improved guidelines.

We aim to keep our preferences as close to industry standards as possible thereby easeing the onboarding process for new developers joining our team.
